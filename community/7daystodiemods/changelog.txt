---

v1.0 - Initial release (July 2026)

SORTING
- One keypress (LeftAlt + X) sorts your whole backpack into nearby
  Writable Storage Crates by the word on their sign.
- 26 category names work out of the box: Ammo, Food, Medical, Tools,
  Books, Resources, Farming, Building, Weapons and more.
- Forgiving sign matching: case, punctuation and plurals never
  matter. "Mod Tools", "Mods \ Tools" and "MOD-TOOLS" are the same.
- Specific crates beat general ones: an "Ammo 9mm" crate wins over
  "Ammo" automatically.
- Overflow chaining: same sign on two crates? Nearest fills first,
  the rest walks outward crate by crate.
- A crate signed "Misc" catches everything unmatched - nothing is
  ever lost or destroyed.
- Containers without a category sign still receive top-ups of items
  they already hold.

RESTOCK
- LeftAlt + Z refills your carried stacks (ammo, food, meds...) from
  nearby crates before you head out.

WHAT IT NEVER TOUCHES
- Your toolbelt, worn armor and equipped items.
- Backpack slots locked with the game's own lock button.

CUSTOMISING
- All rules live in one plain text file (CrateLabels.txt): groups,
  item names, wildcard patterns like foodCan*, and exclusions with a
  minus sign.
- Edit rules from inside the game with the stow console command:
  stow alias, stow what, stow search, stow groups, stow reload.
- Every in-game edit keeps an automatic backup (CrateLabels.txt.bak).
- Hotkeys are configurable in StowItConfig.xml.

LANGUAGES
- Sign labels ship for all official game languages: de, es, fr, it,
  ja, ko, pl, pt-BR, ru, tr, zh-CN, zh-TW.

COMPATIBILITY
- Items from other mods sort like vanilla ones - the item list is
  read from the running game.
- Multiplayer and dedicated server safe: uses the game's own
  container locking, so no lost or duplicated items.
- EAC must be off (like any DLL mod); with EAC on the mod safely
  skips itself.
- Uninstall any time by deleting the mod folder - saves are never
  touched.
