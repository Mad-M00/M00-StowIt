# Restock

Restock (LeftAlt + Z) is the reverse of sorting: it takes items *from*
nearby crates to refill the stacks you already carry.

Carrying 40 rounds of 9mm? Restock tops the stack back up from your
ammo crate. Carrying five grilled meat? It refills those too. It never
gives you item types you are not already carrying, so your backpack
stays exactly as you packed it, just full.

Perfect before a horde night: stand in your storage room, press
LeftAlt + Z, walk away topped up.

## The double press

Press restock twice within two seconds and it also fills empty slots
with new stacks of the things you carry, instead of only topping up
the stacks you have.
